General Information

The list under “General Overview” indicates whether or not a party is populist, far-right, far-left, eurosceptic, and currently in parliament. The table makes use of the following symbols:

  • A ● indicates that a party fulfills a characteristic

  • A ◐ indicates a borderline case

  • At times, the specific time intervals for when the characteristic was fulfilled is provided in parentheses

For country specific reports, click on a country on the interactive map to the right.
